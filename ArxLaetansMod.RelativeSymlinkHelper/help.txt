Place a relative symlink to this folder "ArxLaetansMod.RelativeSymlinkHelper" inside every .Setup folder and rename it to "ArxLaetansMod".
When creating the relative symlink at .Setup to .ftl and textures etc, use the "ArxLaetansMod" symlink folder.
This will help symlinks there to reach the real files during setup/config/release.
